🦷 Tooth Anatomy

The human tooth consists of multiple layers:

  • Enamel — Hardest substance in the body (96% mineral)
  • Dentin — Calcified tissue beneath enamel, contains dentinal tubules
  • Pulp — Vascular connective tissue with nerves and vessels
  • Cementum — Covers root surface, anchors PDL fibers
  • Periodontal Ligament — Connects cementum to alveolar bone
